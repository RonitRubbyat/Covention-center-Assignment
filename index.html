Flower For You

Flower lovers and florists around the world have been using hashtags such as to raise funds, awareness and support for the Ukrainian people in this David and Goliath story now unfolding. It is not only the sunflower, the national flower of Ukraine, that has sparked this support and a barrage of images on the internet, but also interpretations of blue and yellow which are the colors of the Ukrainian flag. For our friend Ukrainian-born Olena Tcaci, who runs Dandelion Floral Art near Chicago, “I have never been so proud of my courageous people. I would like to thank my florist friends who support me with their warm messages from all over the world.”
